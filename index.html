About Us

Founded in 2021

GrocerEase was founded by Andrew Grozdanov after he discovered that his mission in life was to provide affordable access to fresh, healthy food to everyone, no matter where they lived. Easier said than done, right?

He got work.

After months of trial and error, GrocerEase was born. A new way to do grocery shopping was here ‐ all from the comfort of your own home!

Pandemic Proof

2020 was tough. As shelves were emptied in gocery stores everywhere, the COVID-19 pandemic revealed major flaws in the typical, brick-and-mortar grocery store supply chain. Things needed to change. Through an innovative inventory management system, GrocerEase is able to keep high demand essentials in stock year-round. Never get caught off guard again.
